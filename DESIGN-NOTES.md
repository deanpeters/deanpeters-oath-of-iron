**Oath of Iron: Master Project Specification & Content Registry**

This document serves as the absolute blueprint for the *Oath of Iron* digital experience. It contains the technical architecture, visual design system, and the complete content registries required to reconstruct the prototype in any environment.

**I. Technical Foundation & Visual Language**

**1. Design System: "The Iron & Ink Aesthetic"**

The UI is built on a high-tension, cinematic luxury dark theme.

-   **Color Palette (Tailwind Tokens)**:

    -   \--color-iron: \#050505 (Primary Background)

    -   \--color-charcoal: \#111111 (Container Backgrounds)

    -   \--color-gold: \#d4af37 (Primary Accents, Royal Data, Headlines)

    -   \--color-cream: \#f5f5dc (Primary Body Text, Legibility focus)

    -   \--color-crimson: \#dc2626 (Conflict markers, Antagonist Roles)

-   **Typography (Google Fonts)**:

    -   Cinzel: (Weights: 400, 700, 900) - For Authority, Headings, and Status.

    -   Crimson Pro: (Weights: 300, 400, 600) - For Narrative Prose and Bios.

    -   Inter: (Weights: 300, 400, 600) - For Tactical Labels and UI Data.

-   **Core UI Components**:

    -   **The Spine**: A 2px vertical gradient line (Gold/15 opacity) running through subpages.

    -   **The Dot**: A 12px gold circle marking entry points on the spine.

    -   **The Dossier Card**: bg-charcoal with border-white/5 and 1s grayscale-to-color hover transitions.

**II. Character Registry: The Cast of the Kingdom**

*Format: 3-Zone Juxtaposition (Role Metadata \| 9:16 Portrait \| Narrative Dossier)*

**1. Princess Gemria MacKendree**

-   **Role**: THE PROTAGONIST

-   **Label**: Weapon Forged in Prayer

-   **Summary**: A fallen princess of Bonnie Brooke who refuses to break under the tyranny of Prince Lescovarre.

-   **Significance**: She embodies resilient faith, utilizing disguises (cripple, corpse, nun, monk) to survive and retrieve the legal document proving her royal claim.

-   **Asset Logic**: 9:16 Portrait, Period-correct dress (Highland/Monastic).

**2. Prince Phillipe Lescovarre**

-   **Role**: THE ANTAGONIST

-   **Label**: The Usurper

-   **Summary**: A tyrant ruling with an iron fist, justifying cruelty through a twisted "theology of tyranny."

-   **Significance**: He weaponizes Scripture to claim divine right, creating the primary theological conflict as he obsessively hunts Gemria.

-   **Asset Logic**: 9:16 Portrait, Dark Imperial regalia.

**3. Grizmund**

-   **Role**: THE HUNTER

-   **Label**: The Deadliest Asset

-   **Summary**: A clinical, hawk-eyed mercenary hired to track Gemria to the deserts of Sikkina.

-   **Significance**: Represents secular pragmatism. His cold exterior only breaks when Gemria mentions "Davena," a tragic figure from his past.

-   **Asset Logic**: 9:16 Portrait, Surgical/Tactical leather and iron.

**4. Prince Luke MacKendree**

-   **Role**: THE SCHOLAR

-   **Label**: The Spiritual Anchor

-   **Summary**: Gemria’s youngest brother and primary companion during the Act 2 pursuit.

-   **Significance**: A scholarly monk who finds the hand of God in history; he secures the vital family document from Cardinal Graysen.

-   **Asset Logic**: 9:16 Portrait, Monastic robes.

**5. Prince Daniel MacKendree**

-   **Role**: THE HEIR

-   **Label**: Red-Haired Leader

-   **Summary**: The sturdy eldest brother leading the MacKendree family in exile.

-   **Significance**: Struggles with perceived "softness" while his father is ransomed. Leads the rescue at Thrushkill; represents the burden of earthly leadership.

**6. Thomas Falco**

-   **Role**: THE LOYAL KNIGHT

-   **Label**: Devoted Protector

-   **Summary**: A weary resistor acting as a spy and buffer against Lescovarre’s forces.

-   **Significance**: Conflict lies in soul-weariness and secret love for Emily; represents the personal cost of the crossfire.

**7. Sheikh Judar**

-   **Role**: THE UNEXPECTED ALLY

-   **Label**: Man of Mystery

-   **Summary**: Leader of Sikkina and a powerful Saracen raider.

-   **Significance**: Represents cross-faith collaboration; initially a threat, he becomes a vital ally against common enemies.

**V. Functional Logic (The SPA Blueprint)**

-   **Routing**: All navigation is hash-based (\#/home, \#/map, \#/cast, \#/prologue).

-   **View Transition**: CSS fadeIn animation (0.5s ease-out) applied to the \<main\> container.

-   **Interactive Maps**: All location images must wrap an \<a\> tag pointing to: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=[LAT],[LNG].

-   **Dossier Sidebar**: All sub-details use a **3:9 grid** where the left sidebar is md:sticky md:top-24.
